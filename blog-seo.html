SEO Optimizacija — Vodic za pocetnike

Objavljeno: 8. april 2026.

1. Sta je SEO i zasto je bitan?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) je skup tehnika koje pomazu vasem sajtu da se bolje rangira u rezultatima pretrazivaca poput Google-a. Veci rang znaci vise organskog saobracaja — posetilaca koji dolaze na vas sajt bez placenih reklama.

Preko 90% online iskustava pocinje pretragom na Google-u, a prva stranica rezultata dobija preko 95% svih klikova. Ako vas sajt nije na prvoj strani — kao da ne postoji.

2. On-page SEO: osnove koje morate znati

On-page SEO se odnosi na optimizaciju sadrzaja i HTML elemenata na samoj stranici:

  • Title tag — najvazniji SEO element. Treba da bude jedinstven za svaku stranicu, sadrzi kljucnu rec i ima 50-60 karaktera.
  • Meta description — kratak opis stranice koji se prikazuje u rezultatima pretrage. Optimalna duzina je 150-160 karaktera.
  • H1 naslov — svaka stranica treba da ima tacno jedan H1 tag koji jasno opisuje sadrzaj. Koristite H2 i H3 za podnaslove.
  • Alt tekst za slike — opisujte slike za pretrazivace i korisnike sa citacima ekrana. Ukljucite relevantne kljucne reci prirodno.
  • Canonical tag — sprecava probleme sa dupliranim sadrzajem tako sto oznacava preferiranu verziju stranice.
  • Interni linkovi — povezujte stranice medjusobno da pomognete pretrazivacima da razumeju strukturu sajta.

3. Tehnicki SEO

Tehnicki SEO osigurava da pretrazivaci mogu efikasno da pristupe i indeksiraju vas sajt:

  • XML Sitemap — mapa sajta koja pomaze Google-u da pronadje sve vase stranice. Postavite je u koren sajta i prijavite u Google Search Console.
  • Robots.txt — fajl koji kontrolise koje delove sajta pretrazivaci smeju da indeksiraju. Pazljivo konfiguriste da ne blokirate vazne stranice.
  • Strukturirani podaci (Schema.org) — dodajte strukturirane podatke da pomognete Google-u da bolje razume sadrzaj i prikaze bogate rezultate (rich snippets).
  • Brzina sajta — Google koristi brzinu ucitavanja kao faktor rangiranja. Optimizujte slike, koristite kompresiju i kesirajte resurse.
  • Mobile-first indeksiranje — Google primarno koristi mobilnu verziju sajta za rangiranje. Sajt mora biti responzivan.
  • HTTPS — SSL sertifikat je faktor rangiranja. Google favorizuje sajke sigurne sajtove.

4. Off-page SEO: backlinks i autoritet

Off-page SEO se odnosi na aktivnosti van vaseg sajta koje uticu na rangiranje:

  • Backlinks (povratni linkovi) — linkovi sa drugih sajtova ka vasem su jedan od najjacih signala rangiranja. Kvalitet je vazniji od kvantiteta — jedan link sa autoritativnog sajta vredi vise od stotinu sa losih.
  • Social signals — deljenje sadrzaja na drustvenim mrezama indirektno utice na SEO tako sto povecava vidljivost i mogucnost dobijanja backlink-ova.
  • Brand mentions — pominjanje vaseg brenda online, cak i bez linka, moze pozitivno uticati na autoritet domena.

5. Lokalni SEO za Srbiju i region

Ako imate lokalni biznis, lokalni SEO je kljucan za privlacenje kupaca iz vase okoline:

  • Google Business Profile — napravite i optimizujte profil sa tacnim podacima, radnim vremenom, slikama i kategorijama.
  • NAP konzistentnost — ime firme, adresa i telefon (Name, Address, Phone) moraju biti identicni svuda na internetu.
  • Lokalne kljucne reci — koristite termine poput "web dizajn Beograd" ili "SEO agencija Novi Sad" u sadrzaju.
  • Recenzije — aktivno trazite recenzije na Google-u. Veci broj pozitivnih recenzija poboljsava lokalni rang.

SEO Optimization — Beginner's Guide

Published: April 8, 2026.

1. What is SEO and why does it matter?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is a set of techniques that help your website rank better in search engine results like Google. Higher rankings mean more organic traffic — visitors coming to your site without paid advertising.

Over 90% of online experiences begin with a Google search, and the first page of results receives over 95% of all clicks. If your site is not on the first page — it practically does not exist.

2. On-page SEO: essentials you must know

On-page SEO refers to optimizing the content and HTML elements on the page itself:

  • Title tag — the most important SEO element. It should be unique for each page, contain a keyword, and be 50-60 characters long.
  • Meta description — a short description displayed in search results. Optimal length is 150-160 characters.
  • H1 heading — each page should have exactly one H1 tag that clearly describes the content. Use H2 and H3 for subheadings.
  • Image alt text — describe images for search engines and screen reader users. Include relevant keywords naturally.
  • Canonical tag — prevents duplicate content issues by indicating the preferred version of a page.
  • Internal links — connect pages to help search engines understand site structure.

3. Technical SEO

Technical SEO ensures that search engines can efficiently access and index your site:

  • XML Sitemap — a site map that helps Google find all your pages. Place it in the site root and submit it in Google Search Console.
  • Robots.txt — a file that controls which parts of your site search engines can index. Configure carefully to avoid blocking important pages.
  • Structured data (Schema.org) — add structured data to help Google better understand your content and display rich snippets.
  • Page speed — Google uses loading speed as a ranking factor. Optimize images, use compression, and cache resources.
  • Mobile-first indexing — Google primarily uses the mobile version of your site for ranking. Your site must be responsive.
  • HTTPS — an SSL certificate is a ranking factor. Google favors secure websites.

4. Off-page SEO: backlinks and authority

Off-page SEO refers to activities outside your website that influence rankings:

  • Backlinks — links from other websites to yours are one of the strongest ranking signals. Quality matters more than quantity — one link from an authoritative site is worth more than a hundred from poor ones.
  • Social signals — sharing content on social media indirectly affects SEO by increasing visibility and the potential for earning backlinks.
  • Brand mentions — mentions of your brand online, even without a link, can positively impact domain authority.

5. Local SEO for Serbia and the region

If you have a local business, local SEO is key to attracting customers from your area:

  • Google Business Profile — create and optimize your profile with accurate data, business hours, photos, and categories.
  • NAP consistency — your business name, address, and phone number must be identical everywhere online.
  • Local keywords — use terms like "web design Belgrade" or "SEO agency Novi Sad" in your content.
  • Reviews — actively request Google reviews. More positive reviews improve local rankings.
