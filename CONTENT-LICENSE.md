WHY CC BY-NC (AND NOT A MORE PERMISSIVE LICENSE)
===============================================================================

The editorial content of this project takes significant time to write,
translate (SR + EN), and maintain. Much of it (PRIRUCNIK §5 legal
scenarios, blog GDPR articles, ownership verification explanations)
represents concrete experience and analysis that is genuinely useful
for site operators. We share it freely for personal, academic, and
non-profit use because that's how the web gets better.

However, allowing competing commercial scanner services to copy-paste
our hand-written handbook into their own paid offerings would be
unfair. The NC clause exists to prevent that specific abuse while
keeping the content open to everyone who isn't trying to resell it
verbatim.

If you have a commercial use case you think is reasonable, contact us
via the channels listed in privacy.html § 10 and we can discuss
granting an additional license for your specific case.
