      Prava korisnika

      Verzija 2026-04-12-v3 · Poslednje azuriranje: 12. april 2026.

      Ovaj dokument objasnjava konkretno koja prava imate kao korisnik Web Security Scannera i kako da ih ostvarite. Za opsti pregled GDPR prava, pogledajte nas blog clanak o GDPR pravima.

      1. Koja prava imate

      Ako se nalazite u Evropskom ekonomskom prostoru (EEA), imate sledeca prava po GDPR-u:

      • Pravo na pristup (Art. 15) — mozete zatraziti kopiju svih podataka koje imamo o vama
      • Pravo na ispravku (Art. 16) — ako su podaci netacni
      • Pravo na brisanje (Art. 17) — "pravo da se bude zaboravljen"
      • Pravo na ogranicavanje obrade (Art. 18)
      • Pravo na prenosivost podataka (Art. 20) — mozete zatraziti export u masinski citljivom formatu
      • Pravo na prigovor (Art. 21) — mozete prigovoriti obradi na osnovu legitimnog interesa

      2. Sta KONKRETNO imamo o vama

      Web Security Scanner je dizajniran po principu minimizacije podataka. Evo tacno sta cuvamo:

      • SHA-256 hash vase IP adrese (ne raw IP) — ne mozemo da ga "dekodiramo" nazad u vasu IP adresu
      • SHA-256 hash vaseg User-Agent-a
      • URL koji ste skenirali i rezultat skeniranja
      • Consent flag — da ste prihvatili uslove pre skeniranja
      • Ako ste kupili Pro plan: email adresa, license key, status pretplate (primljeno od Lemon Squeezy)

      Nemamo: vasu raw IP adresu, ime, prezime, adresu, telefon, lozinku, istoriju pregledanja. Ne koristimo kolacice za pracenje osim ako eksplicitno odobrite (cookie consent banner).

      3. Kako da ostvarite svoja prava

      Posaljite zahtev na email naveden u Politici privatnosti, sekcija 10. Zahtev mora da sadrzi:

      • Vasu IP adresu u trenutku skeniranja (potrebna da bismo izracunali hash i pronasli vase redove)
      • Priblizno vreme skeniranja (dan je dovoljan)
      • Vrstu zahteva: pristup, brisanje, ispravka, ili export

      Zasto trebamo vasu IP adresu? Zato sto je cuvamo samo kao hash. Bez originalne IP adrese ne mozemo da izracunamo hash i identifikujemo vase redove u bazi. Ovo je posledica naseg privacy-by-design pristupa.

      Rok za odgovor: 30 dana od prijema zahteva (GDPR Art. 12).

      4. Brisanje podataka

      Na vas zahtev brisemo:

      • Sve scans redove koji odgovaraju vasem IP hash-u
      • Sve audit_log redove koji odgovaraju vasem IP hash-u (osim onih koji su flagged=true za pravne svrhe)
      • Sve verified_domains redove vezane za vas IP hash
      • Sve scan_requests redove vezane za vas IP hash

      Sta NE brisemo: redove oznacene sa flagged=true u audit_log-u. Ovo su redovi koji su vezani za potvrdjene prijave zloupotrebe i cuvaju se kao pravni dokaz. Ovaj izuzetak je dozvoljen po GDPR Art. 17(3)(e) — uspostavljanje, ostvarivanje ili odbrana pravnih zahteva.

      5. Kolacici — vasa kontrola

      Sajt koristi granularni cookie consent sa tri kategorije. Mozete promeniti odluku u svakom trenutku klikom na "Podesavanja kolacica" u footer-u bilo koje stranice. Ako odbijete sve osim neophodnih, nikakvi tracking kolacici se ne postavljaju i nikakvi podaci se ne salju Google-u. Detaljniji opis u Politici privatnosti, sekcija 7.

      6. Automatizovano odlucivanje (cl. 38 ZZPL)

      Ovaj servis NE koristi automatizovano odlucivanje koje proizvodi pravne posledice po vas ili znacajno utice na vas polozaj. Skeniranje je tehnicka analiza sajta, ne odlucivanje o vama. Risk score (A-F ocena) je informativni alat.

      7. Pravo na prigovor (cl. 37 ZZPL)

      Imate pravo da podnesete prigovor na obradu vasih podataka koja se zasniva na legitimnom interesu (audit log). Prigovor posaljite na kontakt@gradovi.rs. Ako ne dokazemo da postoje zakonski razlozi za obradu koji pretezsu nad vasim interesima, prekinucemo obradu vasih podataka.

      8. Prenosivost podataka (cl. 36 ZZPL)

      Imate pravo da zatrazite kopiju vasih podataka u masinski citljivom formatu. Na vas zahtev, dostavljamo podatke u JSON ili CSV formatu putem email-a. Zahtev posaljite na kontakt@gradovi.rs sa vasom IP adresom i pribliznim vremenom skeniranja.

      User Rights

      Version 2026-04-12-v3 · Last updated: April 12, 2026.

      This document explains specifically what rights you have as a Web Security Scanner user and how to exercise them. For a general GDPR rights overview, see our blog article on GDPR rights.

      1. Your rights

      If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights under GDPR:

      • Right of access (Art. 15) — you can request a copy of all data we hold about you
      • Right to rectification (Art. 16) — if data is inaccurate
      • Right to erasure (Art. 17) — "right to be forgotten"
      • Right to restriction of processing (Art. 18)
      • Right to data portability (Art. 20) — you can request an export in machine-readable format
      • Right to object (Art. 21) — you can object to processing based on legitimate interest

      2. What we ACTUALLY have about you

      Web Security Scanner is built on a data minimization principle. Here is exactly what we store:

      • SHA-256 hash of your IP address (not raw IP) — we cannot "decode" it back to your IP address
      • SHA-256 hash of your User-Agent
      • URL you scanned and the scan result
      • Consent flag — that you accepted the terms before scanning
      • If you purchased a Pro plan: email address, license key, subscription status (received from Lemon Squeezy)

      We do NOT have: your raw IP address, name, surname, address, phone number, password, or browsing history. We do not use tracking cookies unless you explicitly approve them (cookie consent banner).

      3. How to exercise your rights

      Send a request to the email listed in Privacy Policy, section 10. The request must include:

      • Your IP address at the time of scanning (needed so we can compute the hash and find your rows)
      • Approximate time of scanning (day is sufficient)
      • Type of request: access, erasure, rectification, or export

      Why do we need your IP address? Because we only store it as a hash. Without the original IP address, we cannot compute the hash and identify your rows in the database. This is a consequence of our privacy-by-design approach.

      Response deadline: 30 days from receipt of request (GDPR Art. 12).

      4. Data erasure

      Upon your request, we delete:

      • All scans rows matching your IP hash
      • All audit_log rows matching your IP hash (except those marked flagged=true for legal purposes)
      • All verified_domains rows tied to your IP hash
      • All scan_requests rows tied to your IP hash

      What we do NOT delete: rows marked flagged=true in audit_log. These are rows tied to confirmed abuse reports and are retained as legal evidence. This exception is permitted under GDPR Art. 17(3)(e) — establishment, exercise, or defense of legal claims.

      5. Cookies — your control

      The site uses granular cookie consent with three categories. You can change your decision at any time by clicking "Cookie Settings" in the footer of any page. If you reject all except essential, no tracking cookies are set and no data is sent to Google. Details in Privacy Policy, section 7.

      6. Automated decision-making (Art. 38 ZZPL)

      This service does NOT use automated decision-making that produces legal effects or significantly affects your position. Scanning is a technical analysis of websites, not decision-making about you. The risk score (A-F grade) is an informational tool.

      7. Right to object (Art. 37 ZZPL)

      You have the right to object to processing of your data based on legitimate interest (audit log). Send your objection to kontakt@gradovi.rs. Unless we demonstrate compelling legitimate grounds for processing which override your interests, we will cease processing your data.

      8. Data portability (Art. 36 ZZPL)

      You have the right to receive your data in a machine-readable format. Upon request, we provide data in JSON or CSV format via email. Send your request to kontakt@gradovi.rs with your IP address and approximate scan time.
