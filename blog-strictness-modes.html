      Jedan sken — cetiri nacina da se izracuna ocena. Izaberite profil koji odgovara vasem cilju: od marketing prezentacije do compliance audita. One scan — four ways to compute the grade. Pick the profile that matches your goal: from marketing presentation to compliance audit.

      Zasto 4 nivoa? Why 4 levels?

      Kada smo testirali skener na hardenize.com, dobio je 100/A u podrazumevanom modu — iako je prosao 43 od 66 provera i pao 23. To je bilo previse popustljivo. Sa druge strane, sajt vlasnika koji trazi "100/A za email klijentu" ne zeli isti strog rezim kao kompanija koja priprema ISO27001 audit. When we tested the scanner on hardenize.com, it got 100/A in default mode — even though it passed 43 of 66 checks and failed 23. That was too lenient. On the other hand, a site owner who wants "100/A for the client email" doesn't need the same strict regime as a company preparing an ISO27001 audit.

      Resenje: jedan sken, cetiri profila ocenjivanja. Basic za marketing i brzu prezentaciju, Standard kao V3 default (kompatibilan sa prethodnim izvestajima), Strict kao ozbiljna compliance provera, Paranoid kao maksimum — A samo na 100. Solution: one scan, four scoring profiles. Basic for marketing and quick presentations, Standard as the V3 default (backward-compatible with old reports), Strict as a serious compliance check, Paranoid as the maximum — A only at 100.

Severity weights determine how much one issue "costs". Basic and Standard have diminishing returns (cap on the number of same-severity issues counted); Strict and Paranoid count all.

      Cesto postavljena pitanja Frequently asked questions

      Zasto moj sajt dobija F u Paranoid modu?Why does my site get F in Paranoid mode?

      Paranoid broji SVE kategorije — ukljucujuci SEO, GDPR, Accessibility i Performance. Ako imate bar 3 MEDIUM propusta (−12 svaki) i 3 HIGH (−20 svaki), vec ste na 100 − 96 = 4 → floor=5 → F. Nije bug, to je svrha profila: Paranoid daje A samo savrsenim sajtovima. Paranoid counts EVERY category — SEO, GDPR, Accessibility, Performance included. With just 3 MEDIUM issues (−12 each) and 3 HIGH (−20 each) you're already at 100 − 96 = 4 → floor=5 → F. It's not a bug, that's the point: Paranoid gives A only to perfect sites.

      Da li Standard i dalje odgovara V3 rezultatima?Does Standard still match V3 results?

      Da — to je regresivni uslov. Svaki sken koji je V3 ocenio sa X, V4 Standard mora da oceni sa X. Imamo 23 unit testa (tests/test_strictness.py) koji to potvrdjuju na svakom buildu. Ako se neki V3 izvestaj razlikuje, to je bug i blokira merge. Yes — it's a regression gate. Every scan that V3 rated X, V4 Standard must rate X. We have 23 unit tests (tests/test_strictness.py) enforcing this on every build. Any V3 report that differs is a bug and blocks merge.

      Da li mogu da menjam profil posle skena?Can I change profile after the scan?

      Za sada ne kroz UI — profil se bira pre skena. Ali buduca verzija ce imati re-score dugme u rezultatima (ista lista problema, novi brojevi) jer compute_score() je cista funkcija rezultata i moze da se pokrene ponovo bez novog mreznog skena. Not yet through the UI — profile is picked before scan. A future version will have a re-score button in results (same issue list, new numbers) since compute_score() is a pure function of the result list and can be replayed without a new network scan.

      Da li je Strict mod strogi od Paranoid? Slicne brojke...Is Strict actually easier than Paranoid? The numbers look similar...

      U benchmark tabeli oba mode-a pokazuju 5/F jer vecina javnih sajtova ima dovoljno propusta da pregaze floor. Razlika je u broju detektovanih propusta (Paranoid dodaje Accessibility + Performance kategorije) i u praksi — ako sajt ima jedan CRITICAL, u Strict modu je to −25, u Paranoid modu −35. Na skoro savrsenom sajtu razlika se itekako vidi. In our benchmark both modes show 5/F because most public sites have enough issues to drive the score past the floor. The difference shows in count of detected issues (Paranoid adds Accessibility + Performance) and in practice — one CRITICAL is −25 in Strict vs −35 in Paranoid. On near-perfect sites the gap is clearly visible.

      Gde je zapisano moje podrazumevano podesavanje?Where is my default setting stored?

      Lokalno u browseru (localStorage, kljuc ss_strictness). Ne salje se na server niti na treca lica, ne deli se izmedju uredjaja. Ako obrisete cache, vraca se na Standard. Locally in your browser (localStorage, key ss_strictness). Not sent to the server or third parties, not synced across devices. Clearing cache resets to Standard.
