Cesta pitanja Common questions

+ Kako da se ulogujem na drugom uredjaju? How do I log in on another device?

Nema klasicnog login-a - umesto passworda koristi se vas license key. Otvorite scanner na novom uredjaju, kliknete Aktiviraj Pro, paste key, gotovo. Key je isti na svim uredjajima i nema ogranicenja aktivacija. There is no classic login - instead of a password, you use your license key. Open the scanner on the new device, click Activate Pro, paste the key, done. The key is the same on all devices and has no activation limit.

+ Izgubio sam key, kako da ga dobijem ponovo? I lost my key, how do I recover it?

Kliknite "Upravljaj pretplatom" iznad - to vodi direktno na Lemon Squeezy customer portal. Upisete email koji ste koristili pri kupovini, Lemon Squeezy vam salje magic link, kliknete, vidite sve svoje license key-e. Portal je uvek dostupan i ne zahteva password. Click "Manage subscription" above — it takes you straight to the Lemon Squeezy customer portal. Enter the email you used at purchase, Lemon Squeezy sends you a magic link, click it, and you see all your license keys. The portal is always available and requires no password.

+ Kako da otkazem pretplatu? How do I cancel my subscription?

Otkazivanje ide kroz Lemon Squeezy customer portal (dugme "Upravljaj pretplatom" iznad). Logujete se email-om, kliknete "Cancel subscription". Pro funkcije ostaju aktivne do kraja trenutnog placenog perioda, posle toga se automatski prebacujete na free tier. Nema dodatnih naplata. Vise detalja u Uslovima koriscenja, sekcija 12. Cancellation happens through the Lemon Squeezy customer portal ("Manage subscription" button above). Log in with your email, click "Cancel subscription". Pro features remain active until the end of your current paid period, then you automatically drop to the free tier. No additional charges. More details in Terms of Service, section 12.

+ Zasto nema klasicnog passworda i login-a? Why is there no classic password and login?

Namerno. License key + Lemon Squeezy customer portal zajedno rade sve sto bi klasican account sistem radio, bez rizika password breach-a i bez potrebe za email verifikacijom. Za security scanner ovo je bolja praksa - manje mesta gde se nesto moze pokvariti, a svi operativni use case-ovi (multi-device, recovery, otkazivanje, menjanje kartice) i dalje rade kroz Lemon portal. Deliberate. License key + Lemon Squeezy customer portal together do everything a classic account system would, without password-breach risk and without needing email verification flows. For a security scanner this is actually better practice — fewer places where something can go wrong, and every operational use case (multi-device, recovery, cancellation, card change) still works through the Lemon portal.
